Our vision is to provide a service where you can use a computer or mobile device to request a storage kit, box up your items, have UPS pick them up for you and store your boxes for as long as you like.

You Pack

Visit our mobile-friendly website and select a packing kit to meet your needs. We deliver a packing kit with everything. You pack the items at your convenience.

You Pick

You pick if you want 30, 60 or 90 days of storage.

UPS will pick up your items and take them to our secure location. Only pay for the dimensions of your box, and not some oversized, dark and dusty self storage facility.

You Decide

  • Extend your storage time
  • Get your items back
  • Forward it anywhere like friends and family
  • Donate & Recycle

We Return

At the end of your selected storage period we return all of your things to your home of office.

The Solution

UPS has the trucks, space, and tech. We will pickup your stuff, and make it easy to store and manage digitally with options like donation, recall, forwarding, and selling your stuff.
